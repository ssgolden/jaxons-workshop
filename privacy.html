Privacy Policy

This page explains what information is collected when you place an order.

What we collect

When you place an order, the site stores your name, email address, phone number, delivery details if needed, and the items in your order.

Why we collect it

Your details are used only to process, prepare, and deliver your order, and to provide support if there is an issue.

How it is stored

Order information is stored in the shop database used by Jaxon's Workshop admin tools. Access should be limited to authorized adults managing the store.

Questions

If you need help with your data or an order, contact the shop owner directly.
